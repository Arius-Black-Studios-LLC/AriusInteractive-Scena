Help center

Quick answers for reading, saving progress, and getting started as a creator. For faster help, join our Discord community.

Reading stories

  • Do I need an account? No — you can read published chapters without signing in. An account saves your progress across devices.
  • How do saves work? On a series page, use Save files to run separate playthroughs. Each save keeps its own choices, metrics, and inventory.
  • Why is a chapter locked? Chapters unlock in order. Finish the previous chapter to continue the story.
  • Comments & hearts require a free reader account so we can show your display name and prevent spam.

Your account

  • Log in from the Discover page with a magic link — no password.
  • Set your display name, username, pronouns, and photo on the Account page. This profile appears on episode comments.
  • Creator studio access uses the same login. You do not need a separate creator account.

Creating & publishing

  • Open Creator studio from the header after logging in.
  • New to branching stories? Start with The Conservatory — interactive lessons in the real editor.
  • Publish chapter 1 when it is live-ready; later chapters stay draft until you publish them individually.
  • From Episode details in the graph editor, choose Publish now or Schedule for a date and time.
  • See Content guidelines before publishing.
