Privacy policy

Last updated March 2026. Scena is operated by Arius Interactive (not yet incorporated). This explains what we collect when you use Scena and why.

What we collect

  • Account data — email address (for login), optional profile fields (display name, username, pronouns, avatar).
  • Reading activity — save files, chapter progress, and aggregate chapter-open events used for platform stats.
  • Social features — comments, reactions, and episode hearts tied to your profile.
  • Creator data — series projects, published episodes, and uploaded assets stored in our cloud backend.
  • Technical data — standard server logs and browser storage (local saves when you read without an account).

How we use it

  • Authenticate you and sync progress across devices.
  • Display your public profile on comments.
  • Operate Discover, publishing, and aggregate statistics (e.g. chapters read this week — not sold per-user).
  • Keep the platform secure and enforce content guidelines.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Published stories are visible to readers by design. We use infrastructure providers (such as Supabase) to host data under their security and privacy terms.

Your choices

You can read without an account (progress may stay on-device only). Signed-in users can update profile fields on the Account page. Contact help@ariusinteractive.com to request account deletion.
